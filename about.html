Our Story

VRAI — French for "True"

We believe the best fashion tells a story. Ours is one of authenticity, craftsmanship, and a commitment to doing things differently.

Born from a Simple Idea

VRAI was founded in 2021 with a straightforward belief: fashion should be honest. Honest about where it comes from, how it's made, and who it's made for.

We grew frustrated with an industry built on fast cycles and disposable trends. So we set out to create clothes that feel as good as they look — pieces you reach for again and again, that age gracefully and become part of your story.

Every VRAI garment starts with a question: "Would we wear this five years from now?" If the answer isn't an immediate yes, it doesn't get made.

Craftsmanship Over Speed

Each piece in our collection is developed over months, not weeks. We partner with family-run workshops in Portugal and Italy — artisans who've been perfecting their craft for generations.

Our linen comes from European flax fields. Our cotton is organic and GOTS-certified. Our leather is vegetable-tanned using traditional methods. We choose materials that honor both the person wearing them and the planet they live on.

Slow fashion isn't a marketing label for us — it's how we work.

What We Stand For

The principles that guide every stitch, every decision, every collection

Authenticity

No greenwashing, no inflated markups, no manufactured scarcity. We're transparent about our supply chain, our pricing, and our impact — because trust is the foundation of lasting relationships.

Sustainability

We use organic and responsibly sourced materials, partner with certified ethical factories, and ship in 100% recycled packaging. Our goal is carbon neutrality by 2027.

Inclusivity

Fashion should welcome everyone. We're expanding our size range every season, designing for diverse body types, and building a community that celebrates individuality over conformity.

Quality

Every garment is made to last — through seasons, through washes, through life. We back everything with a two-year quality guarantee because we stand behind every stitch.
